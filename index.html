Feedback From HR EU + MEA December 2025 Meeting

1. The Psychosocial Dimension (Mental Health = Safety)

  • Broaden the Definition of "Injury": Move beyond physical Lost Time Injuries. We must account for long-term sick leave due to stress.
  • The "Early Support Model": Supporting employees at the first signs of stress prevents exhaustion.
